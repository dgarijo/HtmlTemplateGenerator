Introduction back to ToC

Here basically explain that there are many vocabularies for defining metadata properties. In fact, agrovoc (link) collects many of them. The problem is that they are often not highly adopted.

People don't know which properties to add to their vocabulary, and that is why you have guidelines like the one in LOV (http://lov.okfn.org/Recommendations_Vocabulary_Design.pdf) or OBI. These guidelines however, only cover part of the terms introduced in this checklist. We build on them and expand them.

In this document we review which are the most common aspects used to described ontologies. What are the most common properties from vocabularies we have encountered when dealing with Widoco, and which are commonly missing links that should be described.
